Trip Through Ljubljana

Explore the best of Ljubljana in four days

Transportation and Travel

Flight from Madrid to Ljubljana with a duration of 2 hours and 15 minutes. And to go to the center of Ljubljana you can go by our private transport(25 minutes).

Accommodation

Hotel Urban Boutique Hotel: It´s on the center of the city near the castle, the preseren square and the triple bridge, which means you have fantastic views of all the city.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploring the Old Town

Arrive in Ljubljana, check-in, and explore the fabolous Prešeren Square and the Triple Bridge. Dinner at Fari’s, known for Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dishes.It´s important to say that all these things are in our brochure

Day 2: Castles, Parks, and Traditional Dining

Breakfast, visit Ljubljana Castle, where you can enter with 12€ , lunch at Restavracija Most, and afternoon at Tivoli Park, it´s important to bring a sandwich, because you will be tyred.And dinner at Jadro Restaurant for seafood and local flavors.

Day 3: Museums, Bridges, and Bistro Vibes

Visit the National Museum of Slovenia, lunch at Georgie Bistro, and walk to the Dragon Bridge which is perfect to take some picture sfor you instagram. Evening is free to explore the city center, because there are so many and very good shops.

Day 4: Final Morning and Departure

Enjoy breakfast, check out, and do some shopping before the flight home.
