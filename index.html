Trip Through Ljubljana

Explore the best of Ljubljana in four days

Transportation and Travel

Flight from Madrid to Ljubljana with a duration of 2 hours and 15 minutes. Taxi or shuttle transfer from Ljubljana Airport to city center (25 minutes).

Accommodation

Hotel Urban Boutique Hotel: Central location with breakfast included. Modern rooms and walking distance from the old town.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploring the Old Town

Arrive in Ljubljana, check-in, and explore Prešeren Square and Triple Bridge. Dinner at Fari’s, known for Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dishes.

Day 2: Castles, Parks, and Traditional Dining

Breakfast, visit Ljubljana Castle, lunch at Restavracija Most, and afternoon at Tivoli Park. Dinner at Jadro Restaurant for seafood and local flavors.

Day 3: Museums, Bridges, and Bistro Vibes

Visit the National Museum of Slovenia, lunch at Georgie Bistro, and walk to Dragon Bridge. Evening free to explore the city center.

Day 4: Final Morning and Departure

Enjoy breakfast, check out, and do some shopping before the flight home.
